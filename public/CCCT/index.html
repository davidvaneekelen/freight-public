Colonisation Log Parser

What does this tool do? This tool helps System Architects process Elite Dangerous log files.
Simply upload one or more .log files, and the tool will automatically detect any active Colonisation Construction Sites you've docked at recently.
For each project, it shows construction progress, location, and required commodities making it easy to copy and submit your order to F.R.E.I.G.H.T.

Log files are usually located at:
%USERPROFILE%\Saved Games\Frontier Developments\Elite Dangerous\
